Influences

As a child, Yuko dreamed of being a manga artists and admired the way that they integrated dynamic and stylized art with storytelling She draws her inspiration from traditional Japanese woodblock prints.Yuko integrates these traditional elements with relevant topics and subject matters, such as feminism and racial inequality to emphasize their relevance in both past and present.
